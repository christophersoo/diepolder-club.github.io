About Us


Diepolder Club is a social haven based on Roblox (On Tap) primarily for voice chat enthusiasts.

At Diepolder Club, we have a vast variety of signature drinks, allowing both bartenders and customers to explore and create wild recipe combinations.

Everyone is welcome to ponder around the club, socialize with friends, discover secret rooms and special ingredients, and get virtually drunk!
